# RX-Tulasi Solutions – MBA Digital Marketing Project

This repository contains the work completed as part of my MBA project and internship experience with **Rx-Tulasi Solutions Pvt. Ltd.**, focusing on **digital marketing, social media promotions, consumer behavior analysis, and online sales conversion strategies** for e-book products.

---

## **About the Company**

**Rx-Tulasi Solutions Pvt. Ltd.** is a growing digital learning and research-based organization that provides:
- E-books
- Digital courses
- Academic project resources
- Skill development content

The company primarily serves students, early professionals, and learners across India, especially those seeking **affordable, high-quality learning resources**.

---

## **Project Title**
**“Digital and Social Media Marketing Strategies for Promoting E-Books at Rx-Tulasi Solutions Pvt. Ltd.”**

---

## **Objective of the Project**
- To analyze how WhatsApp, Instagram, and Facebook can be used effectively for educational product marketing.
- To understand consumer behavior in the digital era.
- To study the role of reviews, peer influence, and social proof in online purchasing.
- To evaluate practical marketing challenges and strategy adjustments.

---

## **Roles & Responsibilities**
During this project, I practically implemented digital marketing functions:

| Activity | Platform/Method | Purpose |
|--------|----------------|---------|
| Poster Designing | Canva / Instagram | Visual Branding |
| Content Promotion | WhatsApp Groups & Status | Direct Outreach & Relationship Marketing |
| Customer Interaction | WhatsApp & Messenger Chats | Trust Building & Query Handling |
| Social Media Posting | Instagram & Facebook Pages/Groups | Awareness Creation |
| Review Management | Collecting & Sharing Testimonials | Social Proof & Credibility Building |

---

## **Marketing Strategies Used**

### **1. WhatsApp Marketing**
- Direct messaging and follow-up
- Personalized communication
- Status-based awareness
- Group promotion

### **2. Instagram Marketing**
- Posters and reels for attraction (AIDA Model)
- Stories and highlights
- Community engagement techniques

### **3. Facebook Marketing**
- Group posts targeted to relevant interest communities
- Page updates and comment interactions
- Messenger for final conversion

## **Theoretical Concepts Applied**
| Theory / Model | Application in Project |
|----------------|-----------------------|
| AIDA Model | Used to design posters & call-to-action steps |
| Social Proof Theory | Reviews influenced new buyers |
| Customer Journey Model | Awareness → Interest → Action → Retention |
| Diffusion of Innovation | Early buyers influenced later sales |
| Relationship Marketing | WhatsApp personal communication |
